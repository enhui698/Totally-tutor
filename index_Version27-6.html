Totally Tutor
Free Online Tutoring for Underprivileged Students in Singapore
🌈 About the Programme
Totally Tutor is an online, free tutoring programme for underprivileged primary and lower-secondary students in Singapore.
We match passionate university and high school volunteers as friendly tutors for younger students—helping everyone learn and grow together!
Our Highlights
  1. Connect highly educated students to potential kids 🎓🤝👦👧
  2. Use AI to match the best teacher for you! 🤖🧑‍🏫✨
  3. Free while very effective tuition 💸✅📈
  4. Free learning material to help you master your academics 📚🎁📝
  5. Provoke your potential and interest in studying! 🚀🌟❤️‍🔥

A Real Story
Jun Hao, 11, struggled in school — he could only get Math 42 and English 48 and couldn’t afford tutoring. Through Totally Tutor, he was paired with an NTU student for free lessons. Half a year later, his Math rose to 86, English to 74.
